Why do we require emails?

We require students to provide their school email during account registration to ensure the platform remains secure and accountable. By using school email addresses, we can verify who is creating an account. This also allows us to identify users even if they choose to use a fake display name, helping maintain a safe and respectful environment. In cases where inappropriate activity occurs, the associated school email enables us to review and address any concerns effectively. Your email is used solely for identification and account-related purposes, and we are committed to protecting your privacy by ensuring it is not shared with third parties.
